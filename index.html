Welcome! Our research focuses on using genome-scale data to investigate evolutionary processes of speciation, hybridization, and adaptation. Our goal is not only to explore and expand genomic data, but also to create new analytic tools and approaches to enable novel research questions and perspectives. We pursue these questions of molecular evolution and genomics in a variety of organismal systems.
